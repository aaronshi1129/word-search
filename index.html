Word Search

How to Play

  • Click Settings to select a word category or use your custom words
  • Find all words from the word bank in the grid
  • Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Select letters by clicking/touching and dragging in a straight line
  • Found words will be highlighted in the grid and crossed out in the word bank
  • Use the hint button (once per game) if you need help
  • Try to find all words as quickly as you can!
